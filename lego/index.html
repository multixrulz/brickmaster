Galaxy Squad Mobile Laboratory

Usually it’s aliens doing science on humans – in Lego, the humans in the Galaxy Squad do science on the aliens!

The mobile lab carries a mech on the back. It detaches to reveal the laboratory.

The mech has two blasters, one on each arm, and missiles.

The buggy hides inside the cockpit of the laboratory. It has four laser guns and is very fast, but has only one person.

The alien has a scorpion-like vehicle which has two missiles and a laser gun on the tail.

Scuba Nindroid

Scuba Nindroid has red eyes, so he looks evil! He is equipped with two four-barreled gattling guns to blast enemies. He moves around with two turbines, and has three lights for vision in the deep ocean. He has two control panels to control everything. It is all attached to the Nindroid through his helmet, so he can get in and out of the suit fast!
