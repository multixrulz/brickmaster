Scuba Nindroid

Scuba Nindroid has red eyes, so he looks evil! He is equipped with two four-barreled gattling guns to blast enemies. He moves around with two turbines, and has three lights for vision in the deep ocean. He has two control panels to control everything. It is all attached to the Nindroid through his helmet, so he can get in and out of the suit fast!
